Duck Fried Rice

Easy peasy spicy savory fried-rice with duck meat

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Duck meat
  • Kimchi
  • Spring onion
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Chop the duck meat,kimch, and spring onions seperately
  2. Preheat the pan and put the right amount of oil on it
  3. Stir-fry duck meat until it gets enough duck oil
  4. Stir-fry spring onions until it smells good
  5. Add the chopped kimch and keep stir-frying until the kimchi colour gets partially brown.
  6. Add the rice when all the ingredients fully cooked
  7. Contiune stir-fry until all the ingredients are mixed well
  8. Pour the fried rice to an pretty bowl!
