Cabage Nabe

Healthy vegetable and meat soup

Ingredients

  • Asian Cabage
  • Thinly Cut Beef
  • Bukchoi
  • Enoki Mushrooms
  • Soup Powder

Steps

  1. Cut the cabage into square shapes
  2. Stack cabage, beef, and bukchoi in order and repeat three times
  3. Put the stacked layers into the pot
  4. Pour 200ml water and add some seasonings
  5. Boil until the ingredients fully cooked
