Tomato Pasta

Super easy meaty tomato pasta

Ingredients

  • Spagetti pasta
  • Minced beef
  • Pasta sauce

Steps

  1. Boil the water and cook the pasta as your preference
  2. Stir-fry minced beef and pasta sauce
  3. Put the cooked pasta into the meaty sauce
  4. Stir-fry until the pasta mixed well
